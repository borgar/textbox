Support both <b> and <strong> elements.
Support both <i> and <em> elements. As well as <dfn> and <cite> elements.
Support both <sub> and <sup> elements.
Support both <ins> and <del> elements.
Support both <code> and <tt> elements. As well as <dfn> and <cite> elements.
Support both <code> and <tt> elements. As well as <kbd>, <samp> and <var> elements.
Support hyperlinks through <a href=""> although styling must be user supplied.
Support both <abbr> and <acronym> elements. As well as the <q> element.
Can read color from style attributes: Red <span> with a nested green <span> contained inside.
